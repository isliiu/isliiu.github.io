about

during the summer of 2022 as i watched girls and women getting denied access to life-saving reproductive healthcare, i was determined to take action but i didn't know how i could from taipei. with two friends, i created clothes 4 roe, a series of secondhand clothes sales benefiting reproductive rights nonprofits in the united states. i am grounded by a desire to solve meaningful problems through community, whether it's bridging the gender gap in stem or mitigating the harms of artificial intelligence on marginalized communities. over the years, i have been lucky to learn from compassionate and talented people at kode with klossy, girl up, public citizen, global fund for women, the national science foundation, and more. as a creative technologist and activist, i hope to continue building, researching, and advocating for a better world.


some recent adventures:

  • developed philosophical frameworks for military AI
  • redistributed $100k to gender justice movements in east africa
  • sourced and curated outfits for added to the file, georgetown's fashion photography magazine
